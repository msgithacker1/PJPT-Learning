# Interview & Professional Advice

## Interview Questions:

* Extremely likely to ask you about OWASP top 10 attacks. Learn them + understand them.

## Tips & Tricks:

* Use Google. It will be your best friend when Googling and researching tools and how to perform ethical hacks. It's good for finding out all sorts of information. 

## Tools Summary:

* vulnhub.com: has vulnerable virtual machines for you to install, try out and test your hacking skills on.
